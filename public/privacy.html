Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 June 2026

1. Overview

Five in a Row ("we", "our", "the site") is a free, browser-based Gomoku game. This Privacy Policy explains what information the site collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

2. Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data from you. The game runs entirely in your web browser. We do not require an account, we do not ask for your name or email, and we do not track your moves.

3. Cookies and local storage

The site itself does not place any cookies or read from localStorage. The only third-party cookies that may be placed on your device are set by Google AdSense when it serves advertisements (see below).

4. Third-party services — Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You may also opt out of certain third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data from partner sites, please see Google's privacy & terms page.

5. Analytics

We do not currently use any analytics service on this site. If that changes, this policy will be updated.

6. Children's privacy

The site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Links to other sites

The site may contain links to external websites (for example, Google's privacy policy). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when the latest changes were made. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@five-in-a-row.example.com.
